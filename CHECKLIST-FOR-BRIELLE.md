## The big one: your Goldie booking account (~15 min, on your phone)

1. Install the **Goldie** app (free) and create an account.
2. Set your **working hours** — the weekly windows clients can book inside.
3. Add one **service**: "Nail Appointment", **3 hours**, your price.
4. Turn on **text reminders** to clients and new-booking alerts for you.
5. Copy your **online booking page link** and text it to Devon.

Until this is done, the site tells clients to text you to book.
